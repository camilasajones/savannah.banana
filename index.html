My Story:

I was born in September of 2018 in South Carolina. When I was still a little puppy, I was separated from my mom and siblings and abandoned on the streets. I went through fear, cold, hunger, rain and thunderstorms but one day someone finally found me and I was took to a shelther where people gave me love, food and a place to sleep while they would find a family to adopt me. In November, a young and recently married couple saw my cute little face at the adoption's group and decided to adopt me. They lived in Maryland, so I took a ride from South Carolina to Maryland with some of my friends who were also being adopted. I remember my new parents waiting for me. When I arrived my parents gave me lots of attention and took me home. When we got home, I remember that I felt just like home. I was so happy that I couldn't stop playing and making them laugh. Now, I live my dream's life and I'm a spoiled girl.
